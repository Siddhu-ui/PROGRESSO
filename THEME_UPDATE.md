## Tips

1. **Start with background**: Set your main background gradient first
2. **Adjust card background**: Use rgba with low opacity (0.05-0.15) for glass effect
3. **Pick contrasting text**: Ensure text is readable on your background
4. **Choose vibrant accents**: These are used for buttons and highlights
5. **Preview before applying**: Check the color boxes to see your choices
6. **Apply when ready**: Click the apply button to see changes across the app

## Where Colors Are Applied

- **Background**: Main app background
- **Card Background**: All cards, modals, and panels
- **Primary Text**: Main headings and important text
- **Secondary Text**: Descriptions and less important text
- **Accent**: Primary buttons, active states, highlights
- **Secondary Accent**: Gradients, secondary highlights
